Designing digital behaviour change interventions

  • Who -> Teams that developed digital health tools

Designing digital behaviour change interventions

  • Who -> Students at an international university in Germany
  • What -> Exploring needs and putting together a design specification for a digital behaviour change intervention
  • How -> Workshops

Building digital behaviour change interventions

  • Who -> Users (expectant mothers) of a web-app that was developed to facilitate doctor-patient communication
  • What -> Monitor the build to see if iterations (based on end user and stakeholder) feedback were being received more positively
  • How -> Sentiment analysis + User engagement measures of version 1 vs 2

Evaluating digital behaviour change interventions

  • Who -> Users (heart-event patients) of a native-app that was developed to facilitate cardiovascular rehabilitation
  • What -> Compare different missingness strategies with respect to bias and variance of estimates
  • How -> Synthetic data + Simulation

Evaluating digital behaviour change interventions

  • Who -> Clinic that used virtual training to help expectant mothers work more effectively with midwives and doctors
  • What -> Compare different models in predicting costs of patient care
  • How -> Training and Test split
